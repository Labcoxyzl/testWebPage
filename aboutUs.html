About Us

Our Mission

AAAA regulates and develops Applied AI and Analytics in a holistic manner. Through an emphasis on talent, research, innovation, and enterprise, the AAAA creates a dynamic sector filled with opportunities for growth.

Our Vision

AAAA envisions a Singapore which has top notch talents in the field of Artificial Inteligence & Analytics. By working hard, AAAA strives for the best and never settles for less.

Here are some initiatives from AAAA

  • AAAA Award, awarded to students from various education levels yearly
  • AI 4 All, a short exploration course for people to understand AI & Analytics
  • Applied AI & Analytics Crash Course, for adventurers who are interested in picking up the basics of AI & Analytics
  • ...And many more!
